About Me

Hello, I'm Chloe. I am a designer turned developer, originally from the UK and currently living in Atlanta GA. After 12 years of experience in fashion design and 1 year in UX design, I have refined my process of bringing a creative vision to life, while holding myself accountable for delivering all product requirements. I have always loved making things, learning how things work and designing solutions, which is what lead me to explore web development.

I love the challenge of problem solving to balance business and user needs. I have made a career out of this previously in the fashion industry and more recently in the software design and development.
